Cascade semantics. The matching engine assigns exactly one status per PO line, evaluated in precedence order: missing ASN → missing invoice → UoM mismatch → quantity mismatch → shipped-not-invoiced → missing payment → short pay. A line failing an earlier check is never evaluated against later checks — the first failure is the one that matters for triage. Two injector classes (mapping drift and UoM mismatch on the 810 leg) are structurally invisible to the mart by design; their presence is validated by corpus/validate.py, not by per-class mart counts.
